Understanding Monty Hall Problem.

Suppose you're on a game show, and you're given the choice of three doors: Behind one door is a car; behind the others, goats. Chose a door by clicking on it.

SPOILER ::: The prize is behind gate:

Steps:
1 [optional] click on "click to see prize door" button to locate prize
2 select a door by clicking on it.
3 click on "Remove an invalid door" button
4 [optional] click on "switch door" button to switch door
5 click on "see results" button to check results


Optionally, click "Automate 1000 times" to repeat above steps 1000 times (without switching)
